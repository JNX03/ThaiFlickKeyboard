# Privacy Policy — ThaiFlick Keyboard

**Effective date:** April 7, 2026

ThaiFlick Keyboard ("the app") is a Thai input method editor (IME) developed by Jnx03. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

---

## What data the app accesses

### Text you type
As a keyboard app, the app processes the text you type in order to display it on screen and send it to the app you are using. **This text is never recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.**

### Clipboard history
If you use the clipboard panel, the app stores up to 20 recent clipboard entries locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is stored only in the app's private storage (`clipboard_history` preferences)
- Can be cleared at any time from within the keyboard's clipboard panel

### Settings and preferences
The app stores your preferences locally on your device (flick sensitivity, haptic feedback, sound, keyboard height, layout preset). This data never leaves your device.

### Custom key layouts
Any layout customizations you make are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded or shared.

---

## What data the app does NOT collect

- No personal information
- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No advertising identifier (AAID)
- No crash reporting sent to external servers
- No network requests of any kind

The app has **no INTERNET permission** and makes no network connections.

---

## Data storage

All data (clipboard history, settings, custom layouts) is stored exclusively in Android's private app storage on your device. It is not accessible to other apps and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

---

## Children's privacy

This app does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13.

---

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published in the app's Play Store listing and in this file.
